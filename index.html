Who is Olivia Owen?

What a great question! Olivia is an accelerated masters student at OU from Virginia! She also minors in criminal investigation and intelligence analysis.

Why did she choose MIS?

Olivia in high school wanted to major in english but realized it was a bad idea. She researched and found out about MIS and loved it. She sees it as a major filled with language and usefulness. She started as an MIS major and has remained one all through college.

What is her biggest goals?

With a world as big and scary as it is, Olivia wants to make a difference. Helping others with her talents and abilities is what will fufill her life. A life that is entirely selfish misses the true essence of life.
