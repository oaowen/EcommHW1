Elizabeth's tortellini Soup

Ingredients.

3 stalks or celery
3 carrots
one yellow onion
several cloves of garlic (measure with your heart)
4 cups of chicken broth
one can of chickpeas
one can of unsalted diced tomatoes
salt , pepper, italian seasoning (season with your heart)
bag of tortellini

Directions

Sautee carrot, onion, and celery until onions are becoming translucent. Add garlic stirring often so you do not burn it. Add the can of tomatoes to the soup. Drain and rinse the chickpeas and add to the soup. Add the broth. Season with your heart. Bring the soup to a boil. Add the tortelinni and cook them until al dente. Serve in a big bowl and be prepared to want seconds.
