Title Author Genre Rating
The Rose Code Kate Quinn Historical Fiction 5/5 This book follows young women as they serve as intelligence officers for the UK in WWII. The narrative is beautiful and well written. The women in the book are based off of true people. This book also features great minds like Alan Turing
The Midnight Library Matt Haig Fiction 5/5 Matt Haig is a wonderful writter. This book is what got me into his work. It follows a young woman who tried to take her life but instead she is transported to a library. Each book is a different possibility of what her life could have been like if she made a different decision. This book will make you cry and appreciate the life you have. I recommend it to everyone.
The Rosie Project Graeme Simmsion Fiction 4.5/5 I am not a big romance person at all but this book is good. It follows a young scientist who decides he need a life partner. He is on the spectrum so the book follows his individual quirks in getting a mathematically perfect partner, or at least what he thinks is perfect. It is truly a fun and good read.
